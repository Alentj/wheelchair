About Our Smart Wheelchair

What is the Smart Wheelchair?

The Smart Wheelchair is an innovative mobility solution designed for people with disabilities and mobility impairments. It incorporates cutting-edge technology such as **voice control, obstacle detection, and GPS navigation** to enhance independence and accessibility.

Key Features

Voice Control

Operate the wheelchair using simple voice commands.

Obstacle Detection

Uses ultrasonic sensors to detect and avoid obstacles.

GPS Navigation

Navigate to desired locations using GPS tracking.

Emergency Safety

Automatic alerts and emergency call features for added safety.

How It Works

Our Smart Wheelchair integrates multiple technologies to provide seamless mobility. It connects to a **mobile app** via Firebase, allowing users to control movement, monitor battery levels, and even send SOS alerts in case of emergencies.
